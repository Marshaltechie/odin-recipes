Pizza

Description

Indulge in the ultimate comfort food — a crispy, golden crust topped with melty mozzarella, zesty tomato sauce, and your favorite toppings. This homemade pizza combines a perfectly chewy dough, slow-risen for flavor, with a vibrant, herbed sauce made from ripe tomatoes, garlic, and olive oil.

Baked to perfection in a hot oven, every bite delivers a balance of crunch, gooey cheese, and savory goodness. Whether you go classic with pepperoni and basil or creative with mushrooms, olives, and fresh arugula, this pizza recipe guarantees restaurant-quality taste right from your kitchen.

Ingredients

  • 2 ½ cups (315g) all-purpose flour
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 2 ¼ tsp (1 packet) active dry yeast
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • ¾ cup (180ml) warm water

Steps

  1. In a small bowl, mix warm water, sugar, and yeast. Let it sit for about 5–10 minutes until it becomes frothy.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine flour and salt.
  3. Pour in the yeast mixture and olive oil. Mix until a dough forms.
  4. Knead the dough on a lightly floured surface for about 7–10 minutes, until smooth and elastic.
  5. Lightly oil a bowl, place the dough inside, cover it with a damp cloth, and let it rise for 1–1.5 hours (or until doubled in size).
